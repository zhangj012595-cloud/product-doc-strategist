## Data Story

```markdown

## Hook
The one surprising or urgent data point.

## Baseline
Current state and comparison point.

## Tension
What is worsening, blocked, under-monetized, or newly possible?

## Insight
What the data means and why it matters.

## Recommendation
The product decision and expected impact.

## Ask
Budget, team, approval, experiment, or next decision.
```
